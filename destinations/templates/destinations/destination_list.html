Current Season: Nordic Destinations

Choose your next adventure! Each destination features a parallax image and a floating panel with details.

Norway

Travel Dates: Aug 1–14, 2025

Experience the magic of Norway’s fjords, where dramatic cliffs plunge into crystal waters and Viking legends come alive. Cruise through UNESCO-listed landscapes, hike to breathtaking viewpoints, and savor fresh seafood in charming coastal villages. At night, watch the northern lights dance above snow-capped peaks. This is a journey for the adventurous soul!

Sweden

Travel Dates: Aug 15–28, 2025

Wander through Sweden’s endless forests and island-dotted archipelagos. Discover the vibrant culture of Stockholm, unwind with a traditional fika, and marvel at the midnight sun. From the world-famous Ice Hotel to wild moose safaris, every day is a new adventure. Sweden is where nature and modernity blend in perfect harmony.

Denmark

Travel Dates: Sep 1–14, 2025

Cycle along Denmark’s windswept coasts, explore ancient Viking castles, and indulge in Copenhagen’s world-class street food. Embrace the Danish concept of hygge as you relax in cozy cafés and stroll through colorful harbors. Denmark is a blend of history, design, and pure joy—perfect for curious travelers.

Finland

Travel Dates: Sep 15–28, 2025

Unwind in a lakeside sauna, meet reindeer in snowy Lapland, and chase the aurora borealis across the Arctic sky. Finland’s wild beauty is matched only by its warm hospitality. Savor fresh berries, paddle through pristine waters, and discover a land where nature is always close at hand.

Iceland

Travel Dates: Oct 1–14, 2025

Step into a world of fire and ice! Iceland’s geysers, volcanoes, and waterfalls create a landscape like no other. Soak in geothermal hot springs, trek across glaciers, and explore Reykjavik’s vibrant arts scene. Every moment in Iceland is a story waiting to be told.

Coming Soon: Next Season's Destinations

Each season, Nordic Trails curates a new set of countries to explore! Here’s a sneak peek at what’s coming next. Stay tuned for booking dates and new adventures.

Estonia

Coming Spring 2026

Wander the cobbled streets of Tallinn, where medieval towers meet modern cafés. Estonia’s forests and beaches are perfect for quiet escapes, while lively folk festivals bring the country’s traditions to life. A hidden gem for explorers who love both history and nature.

Latvia

Coming Spring 2026

Latvia is a land of music, art, and wild beauty. Explore Riga’s stunning Art Nouveau architecture, paddle through the forests of Gauja National Park, and join in the joy of a traditional song festival. Latvia’s spirit is infectious—come ready to celebrate!

Lithuania

Coming Spring 2026

Lithuania’s fairy-tale castles, rolling dunes, and vibrant folk art make every day an adventure. Stroll Vilnius’s old town, picnic by the lakes, and join in springtime festivals bursting with color and music. Lithuania is a place to make memories that last a lifetime.

Poland

Coming Spring 2026

From Krakow’s fairy-tale castles to the wild Tatra mountains, Poland is a land of discovery. Wander through colorful spring flower festivals, explore ancient salt mines, and relax by the lakes. Poland’s rich history and natural beauty will surprise and delight you at every turn.
